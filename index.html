About Us

Established in the year 2012, Addmart.com has grown and made a prominent place in the printing & advertising industry. "THE OLIVE LITE” & “OLIVE PREMIUM” are the well known and accepted brands of Addmart.com in the market. Company’s vision is to be at the top for meeting all printing & advertising needs. Headquartered in the capital of India, our tremendous growth in the last 10 years has imparted us with confidence, that we have spread our wings globally and the perfect example for that we have an office in Kathmandu and also have branches in Mumbai and Bangalore.

Addmart.com ensures that the quality of our brand is maintained in order to live up to the expectations of the most valuable assets that is our clients. We have always met all the Printing & Advertising needs of our clients in the regions through various sales channels adding up to our rich experience. We have been spearheading in serving some of the top corporate establishments and providing them with quality products at reasonable prices. We greatly value the words of our honourable Prime Minister:- ‘Sabka Sath Sabka Vikas’ and we are working along with the same line for our people, our clients and for our sales partners. OLIVE has over 7 years of experience in meeting customer requirements. We work through various sales channels and have partners, distributors across India in the advertising industry with the aim to achieve 100% customer satisfaction. We provide the best quality products within the budget of our clients.

CSR

AddMart.com strongly believes that sustainable community development is essential for harmony between the community and the industry. The company endeavors to make a positive contribution to the underprivileged communities by supporting a wide range of socio-economic, educational and health care initiatives. AddMart.com is committed to integrating its business values and operations to meet the expectations of all its stakeholders. As a part of its CSR policy, AddMart.com is associated with Mansa Mata trust.

Mansa Mata Trust

Founded in 1910, Mansa Mata Trust has gained more than 105 years of experience with the objective to provide the required facilities for the education of children in remote and slum areas & to empower them for higher studies. The trust holds an exceptional track record of helping children fulfil their dreams.
